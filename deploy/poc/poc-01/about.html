About

Zinnia Labs – Where Artificial Intelligence Meets Scientific Integrity

Mission

Zinnia Labs exists to conduct deep AI research, build production-grade ML systems, and question the dominant narratives around artificial intelligence. We believe the future of AI must be ethical, transparent, open-source, and grounded in science—not hype.

Scope

Our work spans:

  • Generative AI (Diffusion Models, Transformers, RL)
  • Secure ML architectures in the cloud (AWS, GCP, hybrid)
  • Smart City systems, public-sector digital transformation
  • AI philosophy, agentic systems, and governance frameworks

Positioning

Zinnia Labs is not a consultancy. It is not a startup. It is a research initiative and engineering platform aimed at pushing the boundaries of what is possible, necessary, and justifiable in the design of intelligent systems.

Backed By

Zinnia Labs is a division of Zinnia Holdings LLC, headquartered in the United States, with global operations and partnerships across academia, government, and enterprise sectors.
