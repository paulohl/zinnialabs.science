About the Author

Paulo H. Leocadio is an AI researcher, engineer, author, and founder of Zinnia Labs. He leads the intersection of advanced machine learning, cloud systems, and digital transformation with a multidisciplinary lens.

With a background spanning Electronic Engineering, International Business, Solid State Physics, and Data Science, Paulo brings over 25 years of global leadership experience — including past work with Microsoft, AWS, and Smart City projects across Latin America and Southeast Asia.

He is the author of multiple books, including the forthcoming Mastering AWS Cloud and Hugging Face Diffusers, and serves on international editorial boards for AI in Security, Cloud Computing, and Digital Government.

Paulo actively publishes research, mentors emerging talent, and drives Zinnia’s mission to bridge science and implementation through radical clarity and design.
