Behind every framework is a human mind—this is ours.

Paulo H. Leocadio

Paulo is the founder of Zinnia Labs and a relentless researcher in the fields of artificial intelligence, cloud architecture, and digital transformation.

With a foundation in engineering, a master’s in international business, and post-graduate degrees in solid-state physics and data science, his journey bridges the academic, the technical, and the deeply personal.

He is the author of multiple books including Mastering AWS Cloud and the upcoming Hugging Face Diffusers. His research spans Smart City architecture, AI security, and the philosophical dimensions of intelligent systems.

Formerly a Microsoft executive, now an independent thinker. From corporate boardrooms to GPU clusters, from incident response models to reinforcement learning pipelines—his fingerprints are everywhere in Zinnia's codebase and ethos.

Find him writing, diagramming, deploying. Often at 2am.
